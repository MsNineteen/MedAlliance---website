OUR SERVICES

Our company helps finanacially disadvantaged individuals that have been victimized or not treated proffesionally in hospitals and clinics. We help seek justice or the right service to be provided to the inididual involved in the incident.
MedJustice Alliance help bridge a gap between patients and legal services, where we help to take your case to law firms MedJustice Alliance collaborates with and take your case as proBono

Our Mission

Ensure that by the end of 2040, 80% to 95% of the victimized patients have their cases resolved and ensure that all employees and employers of hospitals and clinics treat their patients with professionalism as we know that there are some medical employees that are negligent

Our Vision

The vision of MedJustice Alliance is to give the people the believe that the justice system is also ontheir side whether the victims have influence or not, whether they are monied or not. Everyone deserves equal service delivery
